Who I am
 
 
Thomas Delbergue : Born in 1990 near Lille (north of France).

After a master's degree in 3D digital design at Pôle IIID Roubaix in 2014, and then a passing through the 3D animation movie,
I became in 2015 co-founder of tri-D.
Co-founder and 3D Maker of Trézorium in 2017.
I'm the team's tinker: often called the 21st-century McGyver.
I also help students at Pôle IIID to develop tools with the programming language Python.

My aim: pass on my passions and the use of digital tools (3D modeling/printing, laser cutting, vector drawing, photography, photo editing, stop-motion, video, development of digital tools...) to children and grown children. I'm curious and motivated.
